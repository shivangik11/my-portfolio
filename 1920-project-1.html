Empathize
Hypotheses
~ Taking into account all of the issues that the users were experiencing, I came up with a solution that included the addition of a tracking feature to the website.
~ The core concept was to provide a better user experience to users’
clients by making communication between them seamless.
~ The feature will allow the user’s clients to track the status of their orders in their website.
Defining the Problem
~ Shri Balaji Global is a company of rice miller and exporter,
They have been experiencing trouble keeping track of their orders.
~ The company  take orders over the phone and in person with clients, but after receiving an order they wanted to automate the processes related  to order status, document details, and
payment details.
~ They were looking for a solution where their clients could access
a platform to look for various status updates rather than calling them
numerous times.
~ They wanted to modernise the website with more user friendly features.
